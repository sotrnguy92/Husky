Husky

Siberian Huskies are probably best-known for their incredible sled-pulling skills, but these doggies aren't all business! They indeed make excellent working dogs—they were originally bred to help the Chukchi people of Siberia hunt more efficiently—but they're seriously sweet, friendly, and loyal cuddle bugs, too. From their distinctive, wolf-like appearance, bright blue eyes, and fascinating history, there's a lot to learn—and love!—about Siberian Huskies. Whether you're already a husky parent or considering adding a husky to the family, you'll want to check out these facts about the world's most amazing sled dogs. And let's not forget about the cute pictures, too.
